

## Inter Process Communication
- Allows different **processes** and **threads** to communicate with one another
- Due to having different [[1_Virtual Memory#Virtual memory|address spaces]], a shared global variable will not work

Inter process communication comes in many forms, including
- [[2_Sockets#Binding a socket|UNIX sockets]]
- [[2_Pipes|Pipes]]
- [[3_FIFO|FIFOs]]
- [[4_Message Queue|Message queues]]
- [[5_Shared Memory|Memory mapping and shared memory]]
