## Star Clusters
Stars in a cluster are usually **formed at the same time**, and are **the same distance from Earth**.

The difference in brightness is due to [[2_Main Sequence#Determining mass|mass]].

Plotting the [[2_Stellar Brightness#Apparent magnitude|apparent magnitude]] and [[1_Lights and Colours#Colour index|colour index]] of a star cluster, we expect to see the [[2_Main Sequence#Main Sequence|main sequence]].

### Types of clusters
- **Globular**: oldest and most massive, 1000s of stars
- **Open**: range of ages, 10s - 100s of stars
- **Stellar Associations**: extremely young, contains O-stars, see nebulae around them, 100s - 1000s of stars
